Things I want to Do

  • Interlaken, Switzerland: Hike in the Jungfrau Region, paraglide over Lakes Thun and Brienz, or visit Jungfraujoch, the "Top of Europe."
  • Seoul, South Korea: Explore Gyeongbokgung Palace, shop in Myeongdong, or hike Bukhansan National Park for city views.
  • Paris, France: Visit the Eiffel Tower, stroll the Champs-Élysées, or explore the Louvre’s iconic art collection.
  • Barcelona, Spain: Tour Sagrada Família, wander Park Güell, or relax on Barceloneta Beach.
  • Athens, Greece: Explore the Acropolis, wander the Plaka district, or savor Greek food in a Monastiraki taverna.
